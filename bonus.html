Crimson Pine and Carter

Team Description:

Crimson Pine is a nature-powered hero who draws energy from the mighty pine forests. His sidekick, Carter, is a master of speed and stealth.

Powers

  • Crimson Pine: Can control trees and create natural armor.
  • Crimson Pine: Has superhuman strength and healing abilities.
  • Carter (Sidekick): Can run at super speed for short distances.
